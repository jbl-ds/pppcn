Fabrication Capabilities

Line Bending: Line bending is a process used to bend plastics in a straight line. The line bending process involves heating the plastic material along a line, using a heating element. The plastic softens as it heats, allowing it to be bent. For more information on plastic line bending services, contact us.

CNC Routing: Numerous geometric shapes and curves can be achieved in various plastic materials by using our CNC routing methods. This serves as an advantage when more than simple straight, linear cuts are needed. PPPCN can perform CNC routing on sheet sizes up to 5 feet by 10 feet on any plastic. For more information on CNC routing services, contact us.

Cut-to-Size: Our team at PPPCN have a wide-selection of plastic including acrylic, polycarbonate, and HDPE that we can cut to your exact size specifications. We can cut sheets up to 12 feet and rods and tubes of any diameter.

Digital UV Printing: PPPCN utilizes a cutting-edge, wide-format digital UV printer that can produce a diverse range of prints on a variety of plastic materials. The flatbed design of our UV printer allows our team to produce high-quality prints on rigid substrates.

Laser Cutting: Laser cutting provides the ability to create fine details, curvatures, and complex shapes out of various plastics and can cut them to any desired form. Our team at PPPCN specializes in acrylic laser cutting.

Edge Polishing Irregular Shaping & Beveling: PPPCN offers a variety of edge polishing and beveling for a finished look to your product.

UV Bonding: Our team at PPPCN also offers UV bonding, a light curable, high-strength adhesive that welds together our acrylic products and produces the most optically clear, brilliant results compared to any other type of acrylic bonding.

Fabrication & Assembly: PPPCN is here to help you take your product from the fabrication and assembly phase all the way to production. We have modern, specialized machines along with a skilled team that efficiently execute complex jobs. Our fabrication and assembly process includes concept design, fabrication production and prototype, assembly, presentation, and production.

Bonding Acrylic: At PPPCN we use a solvent-type bonding agent for bonding plexiglass that chemically welds surfaces together. It produces clean, optically transparent cemented joints that result in a beautiful end-product.
